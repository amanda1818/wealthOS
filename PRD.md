# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Sovereign Wealth OS

## 1. Executive Summary
Sovereign Wealth OS is a premium, privacy-first financial command center designed for high-net-worth individuals and ambitious families. It transitions personal finance from basic budgeting to "Sovereignty Auditing"—treating personal wealth like a corporate treasury with a focus on absolute financial independence, liquidity cascading, and passive yield vs. committed burn optimization.

## 2. The Problem It Solves
Traditional personal finance apps (Mint, YNAB, EveryDollar) are built for scarcity—focusing on cutting coupons and micro-managing grocery budgets. They fail to address the complexity of established professionals and families who need to manage:
- **The Independence Gap:** The exact delta between committed monthly burn and passive yield.
- **Complex Household Operations:** Shared expenses, private reserves (individual autonomy within a marriage), and corporate reimbursements (client claims).
- **Treasury Management:** Cascading liquidity (Waterfall method) across multiple strategic envelopes, rather than just tracking past expenses.
- **Vampire Expenses:** Identifying systemic bloat (lifestyle creep, unused subscriptions) that silently drains wealth building capacity.

## 3. Target Customers
**The "Aspiring Sovereign" & High-Net-Worth Households**
- **Demographic:** Top 5-10% income earners, dual-income households, DINKs (Dual Income, No Kids), and affluent families.
- **Psychographic:** Highly driven, analytical, values privacy, and treats their family finances like a business. They want a "Family Office" experience without the exorbitant fees.
- **Pain Points:** Spreadsheets are too manual; consumer apps are too simplistic; wealth managers are too opaque. They need a real-time "Control Tower" for their liquidity.

## 4. Business Model (SaaS / Freemium)
Sovereign Wealth OS operates on a premium SaaS subscription model, reflecting its "Family Office" value proposition.

*   **Basic Tier (Free/Trial):** Essential ledger tracking, basic dashboard, and single-player mode.
*   **Pro / Sovereign Tier (e.g., $15-$25/month or $150/year):**
    *   Multiplayer (Joint + Individual views for spouses).
    *   Advanced "Vampire Hunter" analytics.
    *   "Magic Assistant" (AI-driven financial insights).
    *   "Alpha Concierge" priority support.
    *   Unlimited Waterfall Tiers and Fortress Vaults.
*   **Potential B2B2C Expansion:** White-labeling for boutique wealth managers to offer to their high-net-worth clients.

## 5. End-to-End Pages & Modules

### 5.1. Executive Dashboard (The Control Tower)
*   **Hero Metric:** Total Sovereign Net Worth.
*   **Key Pillars:** Monthly Liquidity (Cash), Private Reserve (Individual discretionary), Total Heritage (Investments/Assets), System Burden (Debts/Liabilities).
*   **Functionality:** A high-level, birds-eye view of the financial empire.

### 5.2. Sovereignty Audit (Gap Details)
*   **Core Logic:** Calculates the "Independence Gap" = (Committed Monthly Burn) - (Passive Yield).
*   **Purpose:** The ultimate scorecard. When the gap hits zero, the user has achieved absolute financial sovereignty.

### 5.3. The Ledger & Intelligence Desk
*   **Functionality:** Advanced transaction tracking (Income vs. Expense).
*   **Intelligence:** Automatically calculates savings rates, net surplus, and categorizes flow. Includes a dedicated desk for tracking outstanding client reimbursements and shared partner splits (Meja Piutang).

### 5.4. Liquid Waterfall (Cascading Envelopes)
*   **Functionality:** Automates capital allocation. Income hits the top tier and cascades down through prioritized envelopes (e.g., Fixed Costs -> Investments -> Discretionary) based on target caps.

### 5.5. The Fortress (Wealth Preservation)
*   **Functionality:** Tracks illiquid assets, investment portfolios, and long-term financial goals (Vaults). Shows Future Value (FV) projections and required monthly velocity to hit targets.

### 5.6. Individual Sanctuary (Private Mode)
*   **Functionality:** A private, secure space for individual spouses to track their own discretionary funds (Private Reserve) without micromanagement from the joint view. Supports financial autonomy within a partnership.

### 5.7. Vampire Hunter & Recurring Manager
*   **Functionality:** Audits fixed costs and recurring subscriptions. Flags anomalies, price hikes, and unused services to aggressively lower the "System Burden."

### 5.8. Magic Assistant (AI Integration)
*   **Functionality:** Context-aware AI that analyzes the current financial snapshot (e.g., "Envelope X is running negative", "You have unpaid client claims"). Acts as a proactive Chief Financial Officer.

### 5.9. Alpha Concierge (Premium Upgrade Gate)
*   **Functionality:** The monetization hub. Highlights locked premium features (advanced AI insights, concierge support) and drives conversions.
